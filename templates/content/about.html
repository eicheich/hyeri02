About Lee Hyeri

Biography

Lee Hyeri (Korean: 이혜리) is a South Korean singer and actress born on June 9, 1994. She is best known as a member of the girl group Girl's Day and for her roles in various television dramas.

She gained widespread recognition for her role as Sung Deok-sun in the hit nostalgic drama "Reply 1988" (2015-2016), which became one of the highest-rated dramas in Korean cable television history.

Following her success in "Reply 1988," Hyeri has established herself as a talented actress, appearing in dramas such as "My Roommate is a Gumiho" (2021), "Moonshine" (2021-2022), and "May I Help You" (2022).
